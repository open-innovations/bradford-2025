---
title: Ticketed
description: Ticketed events in the Bradford 2025 programme are those that require people to book tickets in advance.
---
 
Discounts are available for selected paid events for the following concessions: seniors, Bradford 2025 Youth Pass holders, Access scheme members, under 16s and groups.
Events will be categorised as ‘ticketed paid’, ‘ticketed free to all’ or ‘non-ticketed free’.
