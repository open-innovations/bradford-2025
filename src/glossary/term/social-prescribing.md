---
title: Social Prescribing
description: Social prescribing is a key component of Universal Personalised Care. It is an approach that connects people to activities, groups, and services in their community to meet the practical, social and emotional needs that affect their health and wellbeing.
---

In social prescribing, local agencies such as local charities, social care and health services refer people to a social prescribing link worker who supports them to take control of their health and wellbeing.
