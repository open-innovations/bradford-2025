---
title: LSOA
description: Lower layer Super Output Areas (LSOAs) are geographical areas defined by the Office for National Statistics (ONS) for census statistics.
---

They comprise between 400 and 1,200 households and have a usually resident population between 1,000 and 3,000 persons. 

There is a useful [summary of Census 2021 geographies by the ONS](https://www.ons.gov.uk/methodology/geography/ukgeographies/censusgeographies/census2021geographies).
