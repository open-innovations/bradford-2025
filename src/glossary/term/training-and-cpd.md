title: Training & CPD
description: Training sessions/activities provided to adults as part of their wider vocational training.
---

We will count individual training sessions/activities as well as the number of participants. Training categories will include staff training, work placements, volunteer training (including Community Researcher Training Programme), educational, professional, skills or talent development training provided to the creative sector. 

This can include non-BD residents. See also [participants](/glossary/term/participants).

This can include:
* Wider vocational training
* Enrolment 
* Teacher Training 
* Volunteers in training (Shipley College)
* Community Researcher Training Programme
* Our own staff training programmes
* CPD led by BPH for creative practitioners
* Cultural Fellows
